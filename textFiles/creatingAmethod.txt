For a given method from an interface, i.e GraphAlgorithmsInterface:
    1) Understand what the method does.
    2) Understand/KNOW what the parameters of the method are. DON'T ASSUME what the parameters are, know what they are and the methods they have.
        2.1)***By understand what the parameters of the method are, I mean
        to lookup the methods available to you for a given parameter.
